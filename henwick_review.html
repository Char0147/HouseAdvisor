Full Detials.

The accommodation comprises; Reception hallway with tiled flooring, giving access to the lounge kitchen & first bedroom.
There are views over the garden from the living room and patio doors leading out to access this. From the kitchen is windows looking out over the garden. The kitchen is fully fitted with a range of integrated appliances to include; washing machine & fridge/freezer. The first bedroom is fursnished with a double wardrobe, chest or drawers, desk and chair, double bed with matress and a shelving unit.
From the first floor, the landing gives access to all three bedrooms and the family bathroom, as well as the attic access. The bathroom comprises of a three piece suite, to include bath, toilet and sink.
Externally there is a pebble paved driveway providing off road parking facilities. To the rear are well stocked gardens with a southerly aspect incorporating a patio area, lawn and planted borders.
